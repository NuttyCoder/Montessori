About Us

Little Watsons Montessori provides a nurturing environment for children to learn and grow. Our experienced teachers and staff are dedicated to the Montessori method, helping children develop independence, creativity, and a love for learning.

Programs

Infant Program

Our infant program focuses on providing a safe and loving environment for your little ones to explore and develop.

Toddler Program

Our toddler program encourages curiosity and exploration through hands-on learning activities and play.

Preschool Program

Our preschool program prepares children for future academic success with a focus on practical life skills, language, and math.
