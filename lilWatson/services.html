Our Services

At The Little Watson's, we offer a variety of services to help your child grow and develop. Our services include:

  • Childcare
  • Preschool
  • After School Programs

Childcare

Our childcare services are designed to provide a safe and nurturing environment for your child. We offer full-time and part-time(**if slots are available) care for children ages 3 years to 6 years old. Our experienced staff will work with you to create a schedule that meets your needs and your child's needs.

Preschool

Our preschool program is designed to prepare your child for kindergarten and beyond. We offer a play-based curriculum that focuses on social, emotional, and academic development. Our experienced teachers will work with your child to help them develop the skills they need to succeed in school and in life.

After School Programs

Our after school programs are designed to provide a safe and enriching environment for your child after school. We offer a variety of activities, including homework help, arts and crafts, and outdoor play. Our experienced staff will work with your child to help them develop new skills and interests.
