Discover Montessori

Montessori education is a child-centered approach to learning that encourages children to explore their interests and develop their skills at their own pace. Montessori schools provide a prepared environment that is designed to meet the needs of the child and help them develop the skills they need to succeed in school and in life. Montessori education is based on the principles of respect for the child, the absorbent mind, sensitive periods, the prepared environment, and auto-education. These principles guide the development of the whole child and help children develop the skills they need to succeed in school and in life.

The Five Principles of Montessori

Montessori education is based on five principles that guide the development of the whole child: respect for the child, the absorbent mind, sensitive periods, the prepared environment, and auto-education. These principles are the foundation of Montessori education and help children develop the skills they need to succeed in school and in life.
